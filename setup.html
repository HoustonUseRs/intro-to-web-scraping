Setup

If you've set up for our Intro to R workshop, skip steps 2 and 3.

Mac

  1. Add to Chrome the Selector Gadget plugin
  2. Download and install R
  3. Download and install RStudio
  4. Copy and paste the purple code on the next line into the RStudio console (usually the lower left-hand box), press enter to run
source('https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HoustonUseRs/intro-to-web-scraping/master/scripts/setup.r')

How to find The Iron Yard:

Address
4203 Montrose Blvd #100
Houston, TX 77006

The Iron Yard entrance is located on Colquitt Street. Once you enter the building, turn left on the first floor to enter The Iron Yard. Door will be propped open with a yellow arm chair.

Parking

  • Street parking is available and free (Colquitt, Main, Roseland or Stanford Streets).
  • There are also some visitor parking spots available in the garage across the street.
